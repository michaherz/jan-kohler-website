2. General information on data processing

Scope

We only process personal data of our users insofar as this is necessary to provide a functional website, our content, and our services. As a rule, personal data is only processed after the user's consent. An exception applies in cases where prior consent cannot be obtained for factual reasons and the processing of the data is permitted by law.

Legal basis

Insofar as we obtain consent for processing operations, Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR serves as the legal basis. For processing that is necessary for the performance of a contract, Art. 6 (1) lit. b GDPR applies. Where processing is necessary to fulfil a legitimate interest, Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR applies.

Data erasure and storage duration

The personal data of the person concerned will be deleted or blocked as soon as the purpose of storage ceases to apply. Storage beyond this may take place if provided for by European or national legislator in Union regulations, laws, or other provisions to which the controller is subject.

4. Contact form

On this website, we offer a contact form for sending enquiries to us. When you use this form, the following data is collected and transmitted to us:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

The form submission is processed through Web3Forms, a service operated by Web3Forms (Backend Services Pvt. Ltd.), which forwards submissions to our email address. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Web3Forms servers, processed, and then delivered to us as an email. Web3Forms may temporarily log the submission for spam prevention purposes. We do not store the data separately beyond our email inbox.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR (your consent by voluntarily submitting the form) and Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to enquiries).

Further information: Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

The data will be stored as long as it is necessary to answer your enquiry or fulfil the requested service. In the absence of statutory retention obligations, data is deleted after the business relationship ends.
